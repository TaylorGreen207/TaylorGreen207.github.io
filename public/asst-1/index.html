Let's work together to save our oceans!

  1. The Ocean Cleanup
    The goal of The Ocean Cleanup is to remove 90% of floating plastic in the ocean. The project was started in 2013 and now there is a full fleet of cleanup systems in the Great Pacific Garbage Patch. This cleanup project:
    • Helps to save animals and to create a better environment.
    • Works on engineering, modeling, and plastic pollution research.
    • Plays a role in participating in the largest cleanup in history.
  2. The Pacific Ocean
    Facts About the Pacific Ocean
    • The Pacific Ocean is the world's largest ocean.
    • There are about 25,000 islands within it.
    • The Pacific Ocean is the most polluted ocean in the world.
  3. Why Should We Save the Oceans?
    It is very important to save the marine life within the oceans.
    • A healthy ocean regulates the climate and reduces the impact of climate change.
    • The ocean provides at least one fifth of the protein that humans eat.
    • The ocean produces half of the oxygen that we breathe.
